# Findings

## Privileged Account Management

### Observation
ITAdmin was assigned administrator privileges.

### Security Impact
Administrator accounts have increased access and should be limited to authorized users.

### Recommendation
Use separate administrative accounts and monitor privileged activity.

---

## Disabled User Accounts

### Observation
The Intern account was disabled.

### Security Impact
Disabled accounts prevent former or inactive users from accessing resources.

### Recommendation
Regularly review inactive accounts and remove unnecessary accounts.

---

## Least Privilege

### Observation
Employee1 remained a standard user account.

### Security Impact
Limiting permissions reduces the impact of compromised accounts.

### Recommendation
Users should only receive the permissions required for their role.
